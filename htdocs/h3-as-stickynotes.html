First, we grade how much expertise is required. Easy means none or at most a few weeks of preparation. Medium requires working experience in the field. Expert means, well, being a subject matter expert.

Then, we look at how flexible you'd be in choosing when and how to work. Clocked is the regular day job. Flexible means you can work whenever you want as long as you deliver on time. Free means you don't need to follow someone else's schedule.

Finally, we consider the opportunities to scale up your revenues. Fixed means you get paid by time or quantity, so it's hard to increase your income. Value means you get paid for the value you deliver, but your time is still a limiting factor. Infinity means your time is not part of the equation.
